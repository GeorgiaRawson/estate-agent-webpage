Common Questions for Sellers

How do you determine the valuation?

I combine local market data, recent sold prices, and a detailed inspection of your home’s unique features. I provide an honest figure, not an inflated one just to win your business.

What are the upfront costs?

None. I operate on a "No Sale, No Fee" basis. You only pay for my services once the sale of your property successfully completes at the solicitor's office.

How will my property be marketed?

Your home will be featured on all major portals (Rightmove, Zoopla), social media, and shared with my active database of vetted buyers immediately.

Who handles the viewings?

I do. I believe the agent who knows the property best should be the one showing it. I’ll handle the 2am enquiries and the weekend viewings so you don't have to.
